# TrigramJob
Source code of TrigramJob

To find the 3 words count in the file.

Input:
how are you how are you how are you how are you
are you fine
how are you
are you fine
how are you
are you happy
are you well
hi hello how are you


OutPut:
[ word : are  ,  you , fine ]	2
[ word : are  ,  you , happy ]	1
[ word : are  ,  you , how ]	3
[ word : are  ,  you , well ]	1
[ word : hello  ,  how , are ]	1
[ word : hi  ,  hello , how ]	1
[ word : how  ,  are , you ]	7
[ word : you  ,  how , are ]	3
